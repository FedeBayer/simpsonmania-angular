Treehouse of Horror VII

Treehouse of Horror VII is the seventh horror special

and the first chapter belonging to the eighth season of the animated series The Simpsons, originally premiered on October 27, 1996. In the seventh annual Halloween Special episode, Bart discovers his long-lost twin, Lisa creates a colony of small human-like beings and Kang and Kodos pose as Bill Clinton and Bob Dole to win the 1996 United States elections Joined. It was written by Ken Keeler, Dan Greaney, and David S. Cohen and directed by Mike B. Anderson.1 Phil Hartman did the voice of Clinton.
